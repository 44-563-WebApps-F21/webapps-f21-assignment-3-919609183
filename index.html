OOTY

Ooty is a town and a municipality in the Nilgiris district of the Indian state of Tamil Nadu.
Ooty is a year-round destination, but the ideal time to visit is between the months of April to June and September to November and July to September
The economy is based on tourism and agriculture, along with themanufacture of medicines and photographic film
It's natural environment attracts tourists and it is a popular summer destination.
